Justin Fink | WDD 130

Hello I am Justin Fink and live in Utah, about an hour south of Salt Lake City, with my wife and best friend, Tiffany. I have more than 30 years of experience in IT and technology leadership, with a career that spans consulting, developing enterprise platforms, and leading teams through complex change. Throughout my career, I’ve worked in both hands-on and leadership roles, helping organizations modernize operations, drive digital transformation, and address complex challenges, resulting in measurable outcomes. In my current role, I serve as a Solutions Consultant, partnering with account teams and customer leaders to shape strategy, articulate value, and guide organizations in leveraging the ServiceNow platform and AI to transform their businesses and achieve real-world results.

I’ve always had a strong passion for technology and the way it continually evolves to create new possibilities. More recently, I’ve been actively using AI in both my professional and personal projects, experimenting with it to build applications, streamline processes, and explore innovative approaches to problem-solving. I’m particularly interested in how AI can augment decision-making, improve efficiency, and enable people to focus on more strategic and meaningful work. That same curiosity extends to my home automation projects, where I enjoy integrating technology into everyday life to create smarter, more efficient, and more intuitive systems.

Outside of work, my life revolves around family, church, school, and projects. Tiffany and I have three children. Our two youngest are married, and while there are no grandchildren yet, we’re enjoying this season of life together. Family time, shared experiences, and faith play a central role in my life.

One of my favorite hobbies is tackling projects around the house. I enjoy building things, learning new skills, and seeing ideas come to life. This past year, I completed a 24×30-foot workshop to support future projects, and my next major endeavor is building a covered deck off the house to finally finish the backyard. Whether it involves construction, home improvement, automation, application development, or helping customers get the most value from the ServiceNow platform and AI, I enjoy the process of building, improving, and learning along the way.

At the end of the day, I’m a lifelong learner who values meaningful work, strong relationships, and the satisfaction that comes from building—whether that’s teams, technology solutions, AI-driven applications and agents, or a deck in the backyard.

Utah

Utah is a western U.S. state known for its striking natural beauty, rich history, and strong sense of community. It is home to diverse landscapes ranging from towering mountains and alpine forests to red rock deserts and five iconic national parks. Utah’s economy blends outdoor recreation, technology, agriculture, and tourism, while its culture reflects both pioneer heritage and modern innovation. With a focus on family life, faith, and outdoor living, Utah offers a unique balance of natural adventure and growing urban centers.
