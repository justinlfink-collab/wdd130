Featured Adventures

Half Day Adventure

  • Duration: 3–4 hours
  • Difficulty: Class I–II (beginner friendly)
  • Great for: families, first-timers, team outings

A fun introduction to rafting with plenty of calm water for learning and a few splashy rapids for excitement. Includes safety briefing, guide, raft, helmet, and PFD.

Big Mallard Rapids Run

  • Duration: 5–6 hours
  • Difficulty: Class III (intermediate)
  • Great for: groups, adventurous beginners

Our most popular whitewater trip—bigger waves, stronger currents, and more technical lines. Your guide will coach paddling commands and keep the crew working as a team.

Devil’s Drop Challenge

  • Duration: Full day
  • Difficulty: Class IV (advanced)
  • Great for: experienced rafters, thrill-seekers

A high-adrenaline day featuring steeper drops and powerful rapids. For safety, we recommend previous rafting experience and strong swimming ability.
